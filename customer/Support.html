Frequently Asked Questions

How can I track my parcel? expand_more
You can track your parcel by entering your tracking number in the tracking section of our website or mobile app. Real-time updates are available 24/7.
What are your delivery times? expand_more
Standard delivery takes 2-3 business days. Express delivery is available for next-day delivery in selected areas. International shipping times vary by destination.
How do I cancel my booking? expand_more
You can cancel your booking up to 2 hours before the scheduled pickup time through your dashboard. Cancellation fees may apply depending on the timing.
